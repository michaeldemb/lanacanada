What We Do
Comprehensive Immigration Services

Whether you're planning your move, applying for temporary status, or ready for permanent residency — we provide expert guidance at every stage.

01

Strategy & Planning

A successful immigration journey begins with a solid plan. We develop a personalized roadmap based on your unique profile, goals, and timeline.

  • Before Arrival — Eligibility assessment, program selection, document preparation, and a clear step-by-step plan before you set foot in Canada.
  • After Arrival — Ongoing support with status maintenance, next steps toward PR, and adapting your strategy as programs evolve.
02

Temporary Residence

Need to visit, study, or work in Canada? We handle the full application process for temporary status and any extensions you may need.

  • Visitor Visas & Super Visas
  • Study Permits & Extensions
  • Work Permits (open & employer-specific)
  • Status Extensions & Renewals
  • Restoration of Status
03

Permanent Residency

Ready to make Canada your home? We guide you through every PR pathway to find the one that fits your profile and maximizes your chances.

  • Express Entry (FSW, CEC, FST)
  • Provincial Nominee Programs (PNP)
  • Atlantic Immigration Program
  • Spousal & Family Sponsorship
  • Humanitarian & Compassionate cases
