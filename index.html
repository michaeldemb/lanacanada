What We Do
How we help

Planning a move, applying for a visa, or going for permanent residency? Here's what we cover.

01

Strategy & Planning

Immigration works best when you have a plan before you start. We look at your background, goals, and timeline, then map out the steps.

  • Before Arrival — We check your eligibility, pick the right program, prep your documents, and lay out exactly what happens next.
  • After Arrival — We help you maintain your status, plan your next steps toward PR, and adjust course when programs change.
02

Temporary Residence

Need to visit, study, or work in Canada? We handle the full application process for temporary status and any extensions you may need.

  • Visitor Visas & Super Visas
  • Study Permits & Extensions
  • Work Permits (open & employer-specific)
  • Status Extensions & Renewals
  • Restoration of Status
03

Permanent Residency

If you want to stay in Canada permanently, we help you figure out which PR pathway actually fits your situation and walk you through the application.

  • Express Entry (FSW, CEC, FST)
  • Provincial Nominee Programs (PNP)
  • Atlantic Immigration Program
  • Spousal & Family Sponsorship
  • Humanitarian & Compassionate cases
