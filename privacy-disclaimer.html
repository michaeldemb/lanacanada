Confidentiality & Data Handling

Privacy Statement

Lana Immigration recognizes the importance of private information and its sensitivity.

Please note that as an immigration consultant, we have a professional responsibility to keep information confidential, in accordance with CICC regulations.

You choose us to represent you based on trust and professionalism, and it is extremely important to us to maintain that relationship.

We are obliged by CICC regulations and bylaws to keep our client files and records even after the end of the retainer. Our company stores and maintains personal information in conformity with the requirements of the CICC regulations and bylaws — and in specific, the CICC Client File Management Regulation.

Your personal information may only be disclosed in the following circumstances:

  • When it is required by a Canadian court to do so — for example, if a judge issues a subpoena
  • When the immigration services rendered under a retainer agreement require us to share some or all client information with CICC, CBSA, ESDC, or other relevant third parties
  • When it has been requested or consented to by the client or prospect
  • If we engage with a third party for administrative services and that third party is bound by our privacy policy
  • When it is required to establish or collect fees
  • If the information is already publicly known

Electronic Communication

Our website may contain links to official and verified websites. However, even in those cases, we cannot guarantee the privacy policies of those external sites.

Email and other communication platforms used by our company — such as WhatsApp, Facebook, and others — are not 100% secured platforms. You should be mindful of this when contacting us through these channels.
